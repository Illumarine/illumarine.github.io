What is Illumarine?

illumos-Based

Illumarine is a desktop-focused operating system based on the illumos (OpenSolaris) family of operating systems

User Oriented

We aim to make Illumarine as easy to use as humanly possible. We do this through ports of existing software, and making our own.

Community Driven

No corporate entity decides what happens to Illumarine. We are fully independent, and 100% built by the community.
